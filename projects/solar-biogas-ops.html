Solar and Biogas Operations Improvement

Internship | Halo Green Energie, Hyderabad (2019-2021)

Context

Worked on operational improvement in renewable energy projects including solar and biogas plant systems.

Problem

Existing system setups had improvement opportunities in power output efficiency and energy-loss reduction.

Methods

  • Reviewed operational configuration and orientation strategy in solar applications.
  • Contributed process improvement ideas for biogas and plant energy transfer mechanisms.
  • Evaluated sustainability opportunities, including byproduct reuse pathways.

Outcome

Proposed solar panel orientation changes reported to increase output by more than 5 percent and contributed additional ideas to improve biogas system efficiency.

My Role

Participated in operational analysis, improvement ideation, and sustainability-focused recommendation discussions within field implementation constraints.
