A Village Awakens: Farmer-to-Supplier Platform

Social Impact Initiative

Context

Built a website initiative to support farmers with direct access to trusted agricultural input suppliers.

Problem

Farmers in the target ecosystem faced information asymmetry, supplier trust issues, and exploitation risks in purchasing seed, fertilizer, and pesticide inputs.

Methods

  • Defined user needs and barriers in supplier connection workflows.
  • Developed platform logic for direct supplier linkage and transaction support.
  • Added feedback and grievance mechanisms to improve trust and usability.

Outcome

The initiative supported transactions totaling approximately 1 million INR while improving access quality and decision confidence for participating farmers.

My Role

Led concept and platform development with a focus on user trust, process clarity, and practical adoption.
